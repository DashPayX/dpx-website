About

What is DashPayX?

DashPayX is a payments-first crypto asset with built-in staking rewards. The goal is simple: make sending and earning crypto as easy as sending a WhatsApp message.

Payments

Fast, low-fee transfers

DPX is designed for everyday payments — peer-to-peer, merchant, and cross-border use cases — with near-instant finality and low gas costs.

Staking

Simple staking rewards

Stake DPX in a straightforward staking contract. No leverage, no complex strategies. Just transparent on-chain rewards.

Vision

Emerging-markets first

Starting from Pakistan, UAE & GCC, DashPayX aims to become a trusted rails layer for everyday crypto payments and remittances.

Staking

How DPX staking works (testnet)

A simple staking contract already lives on BNB Chain Testnet. Stake DPX, track your rewards, and understand the mechanics before any real mainnet deployment.

1. Stake DPX

You deposit your DPX in a non-custodial staking contract. Your tokens stay on-chain; you can exit anytime (no lock-in on testnet).

2. Earn rewards

Rewards accrue block-by-block based on your share of the staking pool. The model can later be tuned using on-chain parameters.

3. Claim or exit

You can claim rewards without unstaking, or exit fully by withdrawing principal + rewards. Everything is transparent on explorer.

FAQ

Important disclaimers & answers

Is DPX live on mainnet?

Not yet. Right now DashPayX is running as a prototype on BNB Smart Chain Testnet. This is a learning and experimentation phase — not a live product for real money.

Is this financial advice or a guarantee of profit?

No. Nothing here is financial advice or a promise of returns. Crypto is high-risk. You should never invest money you cannot afford to lose, and you should always do your own research.

Who is behind DashPayX?

DashPayX is currently a small, focused initiative exploring a serious payments-plus-staking model, starting from South Asia and the Gulf region. The project will evolve transparently as community and contributions grow.

Will there be audits and KYC?

Before any real mainnet launch or fundraising, formal smart-contract audits and appropriate compliance steps are planned. Testnet is specifically for learning, feedback and proving the design.
