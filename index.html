About DashPayX

Clean token. Real utility. No gimmicks.

DashPayX is designed for users who are tired of complex, tax-heavy, hype-driven tokens. DPX keeps things simple: clean tokenomics, staking, and payments.

DPX is a fixed-supply BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain. There are no transfer taxes, no blacklists, and no hidden mint functions — just transparent, auditable code.

Built for Pakistan & GCC markets where simple, transparent crypto usage is still scarce.
Staking as the first core product, followed by payments and loyalty-style use cases.
No presale, no private round noise — the focus is on fair launch and long-term ecosystem growth.
Principles of DPX:
  • ✅ Fixed supply & transparent contracts
  • ✅ No reflections, no tax complexity
  • ✅ No hidden admin “magic” functions
  • ✅ Focus on utility: staking + payments
  • ✅ Step-by-step, sustainable rollout

DPX is intentionally simple — so users, partners, and auditors can understand the mechanics without decoding complicated token logic.

Launch Plan

Roadmap & Phases

DashPayX is rolling out in clear, accountable phases — from testnet and branding to staking, payments, and regional adoption.

Phase 1
Testnet Foundations
Deploy DPX token and staking contracts on BNB Chain testnet. Validate full staking cycle (stake, earn, claim, withdraw).
Phase 2
Brand & Website
Finalize visual identity, website v2, basic documentation, and official social channels (Twitter, Telegram).
Phase 3
Mainnet Token Deployment
Deploy DPX on BNB Chain mainnet, configure ownership and allocations, and prepare for initial liquidity provisioning.
Phase 4
Liquidity & LP Lock
Add liquidity on PancakeSwap, lock LP tokens via a reputable locker, and publish all relevant links for full transparency.
Phase 5
Staking Front-End
Launch a simple, clear staking interface for DPX holders to lock tokens and earn rewards with no complex gas gymnastics.
Phase 6
Payments & Utility Pilots
Begin experiments and pilot programs for using DPX as a payment or rewards layer in Pakistan and GCC ecosystems.
The roadmap is deliberately focused and realistic: testnet first, then branding, then mainnet and staking, followed by actual usage and integrations. No grand promises — only steps we can deliver, measure, and improve.
Staking Preview

Staking. Simple, transparent, and on-chain.

The DPX staking contract is already tested on testnet. The mainnet version will keep the same philosophy: clear rewards, clear rules, and no hidden behavior.

The DPX staking experience is designed to be straightforward: connect your wallet, choose your amount, stake, and watch rewards accrue. No complicated lock-ins by default — flexible models can be added over time.

Staking parameters such as reward rate and minimum stake will be configurable by the protocol owner, with all settings visible on-chain and in the front-end.
