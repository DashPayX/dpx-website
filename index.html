About DPX

A clean, utility-driven token

DashPayX avoids confusing token tricks. No reflections, no rebasing, no hidden mints. Just a fixed-supply token with staking and a clear payments-first roadmap.

Payments-first

Built for everyday transfers

DPX is designed for peer-to-peer, merchant and cross-border use on BNB Smart Chain. Low fees and fast confirmation make it practical for real-world usage in Pakistan, UAE, GCC and beyond.

Simple staking

Earn without complexity

Holders can stake DPX into a non-custodial contract, track rewards, and withdraw at any time (based on the live parameters). No leverage, no hidden risk layers.

Long-term vision

From token to payments stack

Over time, DashPayX aims to evolve into a payments + loyalty stack: merchant tools, simple dashboards, and potential governance features once the community grows.

Utility

Three core roles for DPX

At launch, DPX focuses on two main utilities — payments and staking. Governance is planned as a future layer once there is a strong active community.

1. Payments

Fast value transfer

Use DPX to send value worldwide in seconds. BNB Chain offers low fees and fast blocks, making DPX practical for everyday use, remittances and digital commerce.

2. Staking

Earn rewards on-chain

A portion of the 1B supply (40%) is dedicated to staking rewards. As more users stake, the reward rate and parameters can be tuned transparently via on-chain configuration.

3. Governance (future)

Community-driven evolution

Over time, DPX holders may be able to vote on upgrades, reward parameters and treasury usage. Governance will be introduced only after strong product and community foundations are in place.

Tokenomics

1B fixed supply, clear allocations

DashPayX is planned with a 1,000,000,000 DPX mainnet supply. No buy/sell tax, no hidden mints, and a transparent allocation split for rewards, liquidity, treasury, team and future utilities.

Bucket Allocation Purpose
Staking Rewards 40% (400M) Long-term incentives for DPX stakers, distributed gradually over several years through the staking contract.
Liquidity & Market-Making 20% (200M) Initial DEX liquidity on PancakeSwap and future CEX market-making or liquidity reinforcement if needed.
Treasury & Marketing 20% (200M) Listings, partnerships, community incentives, operations, and development under a transparent treasury framework.
Team (Vested) 10% (100M) Time-locked allocation for the core team and key contributors, aligned with long-term growth rather than short-term speculation.
Future Utilities / CEX / Strategic 10% (100M) Reserved for future products, payment utilities, strategic deals and potential centralized exchange integrations.

Design principles

  • No stealth taxes, reflections or rebasing.
  • Manageable supply that is easy to reason about.
  • Rewards focused on long-term staking and ecosystem support, not short-term hype.
  • Enough flexibility to grow into a full payments + loyalty stack over time.
Roadmap

From testnet to real-world usage

DashPayX is being built in phases: first a working testnet prototype, then a careful mainnet launch, followed by merchant and ecosystem integrations.

Phase 1

Architecture & testnet

Finalize the DPX token and staking contracts, deploy to BNB Chain Testnet, and validate the full staking cycle with real users before any mainnet launch.

Phase 2

Mainnet & liquidity

Launch DPX on BNB Chain mainnet with a fixed 1B supply, provide initial PancakeSwap liquidity, lock LP, and open staking with clearly communicated parameters and audits within budget.

Phase 3

Payments & ecosystem

Roll out a simple payments stack, merchant tools, potential loyalty features, and community-driven governance, starting in Pakistan and GCC markets.

Not yet. At this stage, DashPayX is live on BNB Chain Testnet so that staking and flows can be tested safely. The 1B supply and tokenomics described here are for the planned mainnet launch.

No. Nothing on this site is financial advice. Crypto assets are risky and extremely volatile. Never invest money you cannot afford to lose and always do your own research.

Will smart contracts be reviewed?

Before any mainnet deployment, the contracts are intended to undergo a light-weight review / audit within a realistic budget, focusing on safety and clarity rather than hype.

Who is behind DashPayX?

DashPayX is an emerging, focused initiative exploring serious crypto payments plus staking. The project will be built transparently, with community channels and clear updates as it evolves.
