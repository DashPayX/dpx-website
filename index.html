About DashPayX

A protocol for simple, clean value transfer.

DashPayX exists because most tokens are built for speculation first and utility later. DPX reverses that priority, focusing on staking and payments for real people and businesses across Pakistan & the Gulf.

The crypto space is full of complex tokenomics, hidden taxes, and projects that never move beyond hype. For users in Pakistan and the GCC, this makes everyday crypto usage confusing and risky.

DashPayX is a simple, utility-first BEP-20 token: no tax, no reflections, no stealth mints. Just a fixed supply, a staking layer, and a payment-focused roadmap that can be understood without decoding an overly clever smart contract.

By building on BNB Smart Chain, DPX leverages low fees, high throughput, and broad infrastructure support — making it practical for everyday transfers, micro-payments, and future merchant integrations.

Designed for individuals, freelancers, and merchants who need a predictable medium of exchange.

How to Buy

How to buy DashPayX (DPX) on BSC.

Buying DPX follows the same flow as most BEP-20 tokens on BNB Smart Chain. Always verify that you are using the official contract address and official links.

Step 1
Install a Web3 wallet

Install a wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet. Keep your seed phrase offline and never share it with anyone — not even the team.

Step 2
Add BNB Smart Chain

Configure BNB Smart Chain (BSC) in your wallet using the official RPC and chain ID (56 for mainnet). Many wallets now include BSC by default, but always double-check settings.

Step 3
Fund with BNB

Transfer BNB to your wallet for both the token purchase and gas fees. Use exchanges or bridges that you trust.

Step 4
Swap BNB → DPX

Go to PancakeSwap, connect your wallet, and paste the official DPX contract address. Double-check that the token you see matches the official contract before swapping.

Important: Always verify the DPX contract address from the official website or announcement channels before buying. Never rely on random search results or unsolicited messages.

Never share your seed phrase or private keys. The DashPayX team will never DM you first or ask for your seed phrase.

What is DashPayX (DPX)?
+
DPX is a BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. It is built around two core utilities: staking and everyday payments, with a specific focus on users and businesses in Pakistan & the GCC.
Is DPX a meme coin?
+
No. DashPayX is a utility-focused token. There is no meme narrative, no inflationary reward loop, and no built-in gambling mechanics. The intent is to function as a simple staking and payment asset.
Is there any buy or sell tax?
+
No. DPX transfers have 0% buy tax and 0% sell tax. There are no reflections or embedded fee routes inside the token contract.
How is the liquidity protected?
+
Liquidity is planned to be locked via a recognized LP locker. The lock details (duration, amount, and links) will be shared publicly so that holders can verify them on-chain.
Is DashPayX audited?
+
A formal code review and/or audit is part of the roadmap. Even after an initial audit, the project aims to treat security as an ongoing process — not a single event.
What are the main risks?
+
Crypto assets are highly volatile, and smart contracts can contain bugs. There is no guarantee that DPX will retain value or that adoption targets will be met. Never invest money you cannot afford to lose.
